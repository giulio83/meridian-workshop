# Executive Summary

**Proposal in response to RFP #MC-2026-0417**
Meridian Components, Inc. — Inventory Dashboard Modernization

---

Meridian Components has a working inventory dashboard and an operations team that can't fully use it. The Reports module has accumulated at least eight known defects. A new Restocking feature has been requested but never built. And a lack of automated test coverage has led IT to block all changes — creating a situation where the system can't improve without risk, and can't stay still without falling further behind.

We have reviewed the RFP, the previous vendor's handoff documentation, and the existing codebase. This proposal is scoped to the real system.

**Our approach is sequenced to unblock work first.** We will establish automated end-to-end test coverage in the opening weeks of the engagement — before touching the Reports bugs, before building the Restocking view. This gives Meridian IT a clear signal to approve changes and gives us a safety net for everything that follows.

From there, we will work through the required scope in priority order: remediate the Reports module (R1), build the Restocking recommendations view (R2), and deliver current-state architecture documentation suitable for IT handoff (R4). Desired enhancements — UI modernization, expanded internationalization, and dark mode — are scoped into a third phase with an explicit go/no-go decision point after Phase 2.

**Delivery is fixed-fee over eight weeks.** Required scope (R1–R4) is priced at $38,500. Desired features (D1–D3) are available as a $12,000 add-on, with the decision deferred to week six when we have real progress data to plan against.

We are ready to begin the week of May 12, 2026.
